üìä Key Insights

Revenue Growth: Driven by a small set of categories and regions.

Delivery Performance: Delays strongly impact customer ratings; on-time % dips during high-volume periods.

Seller Risk: Minority of sellers responsible for majority of SLA breaches. Stars set benchmarks; At Risk sellers need targeted intervention.

Freight Cost: Distance and category influence shipping cost; high-distance categories have optimization potential.

Customer Experience: Timely delivery directly correlates with satisfaction; delayed orders consistently lower ratings.
